PowerShell Saturday

We are trying to organize a PowerShell Saturday (a one-day, small-format technical event) in the Chicago area. We are considering holding the event in October 2018 but this may change.

We are in the early stages of planning the event and would like to gauge attendee interest. We have been in contact with 4 speakers that have expressed interest in presenting if the event date fits into their schedules. We are seeking additional speakers so we can have 1 track with 6 sessions (9am to 5pm). If you are interested in attending, presenting or assisting with organizing the event, please join the mailing list below. More details will be provided via email, and this website, when there is progress to report.
